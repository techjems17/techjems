Get to know the real me

I was born and raised in Colorado Springs, and moved a bit north to Monument six years ago. I am the second child of four; I have an older sister, a younger sister, and a younger brother. My hobbies are drawing, doing puzzles, and doing outdoor activities. During my sophomore year of high school I found I was bored. My Mother was informed of an accelerated program, where I could go to college if I tested high enough. I graduated high school in 2014 and college with my Bachelors of science in 2015. My original goal in life was to become a doctor, but over the past few years I have changed my career path. About six months ago I got a job in as a medical technician. This job helped me realize I do not like the medical field, mainly because I did not enjoy the lack of free will. I decided that I needed to take a step back and look at my life. I analyzed my life and found Galvanize, and now I am on a journey to change my life.
